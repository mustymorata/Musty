Academic Course Registration Guide

HTML5 Core Materialize Framework UI/UX Design

A specialized static portal prototype made for 100 & 200 Level Computer Science students at Northwest University, Kano, simplifying registration procedures.

Functional Overview

Newly admitted university students often encounter bottlenecks navigating academic listings and prerequisites. This project resolves that issue by delivering a clean, lightweight directory displaying requisite courses, registration timelines, and department contact info.

Design Integrity

Built exclusively on foundational CSS standards combined with the Google Materialize design engine. Every page relies on semantic elements to remain highly accessible and incredibly fast, even when loaded over slower mobile connections.

Project Outcomes

By streamlining academic registration guidelines into single-scroll structures, the prototype demonstrates how intentional frontend design can address major systemic communication challenges.
